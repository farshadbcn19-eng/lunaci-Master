Our Story · Nuestra Historia

The Beginning

El Comienzo

LUNACI Barcelona was born from a philosophy — a deep appreciation for beauty as an essential element of human experience. From the beginning, our ambition was larger than simply creating another name within the beauty industry.

LUNACI Barcelona nació de una filosofía — una profunda apreciación por la belleza como elemento esencial de la experiencia humana.

The dream behind LUNACI was to create a complete universe of beauty. A universe where quality, aesthetics, craftsmanship and personal expression coexist within a single coherent philosophy. A universe where beauty is not reduced to appearance but elevated into an experience.

El sueño detrás de LUNACI era crear un universo completo de belleza — donde la calidad, la estética y la expresión personal coexisten en una filosofía coherente.

Rather than pursuing visibility for its own sake, we focused on creating meaningful experiences. Rather than defining beauty through external standards, we chose to celebrate individuality. Rather than following every passing trend, we built an identity rooted in enduring values — confidence, authenticity, elegance, quality and memorable presence.

En lugar de perseguir la visibilidad por sí misma, nos centramos en crear experiencias significativas y celebrar la individualidad de cada mujer.

Why LUNACI Exists

Beauty should support self-expression. It should strengthen confidence. It should enhance quality of life. And it should allow individuals to present the most authentic version of themselves. This is not simply a philosophy — it is the reason LUNACI was created.

La belleza debe apoyar la autoexpresión, fortalecer la confianza y permitir que cada individuo presente la versión más auténtica de sí mismo. Esta es la razón por la que LUNACI fue creada.

01

Confidence

Confianza

Every product, campaign and communication should strengthen confidence. True confidence is built from within rather than granted by external approval. LUNACI exists to support the confidence that already lives within every woman.

Cada producto y comunicación debe fortalecer la confianza que ya vive dentro de cada mujer.

02

Authenticity

Autenticidad

The brand must remain genuine, honest and emotionally credible in everything it creates. We celebrate individuality rather than imposing standards. Beauty should never become a source of pressure — it should be a source of freedom.

La marca debe permanecer genuina y honesta. Celebramos la individualidad en lugar de imponer estándares.

03

Elegance

Elegancia

Luxury should be refined, intentional and timeless. True elegance never requires excess — it emerges from thoughtful restraint, careful craftsmanship and the wisdom to know that less, when done perfectly, is always more.

El lujo debe ser refinado e intemporal. La verdadera elegancia nunca requiere exceso — emerge de la contención cuidadosa.
